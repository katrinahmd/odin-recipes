Vegetarian Sausage Egg Muffin

Description

This recipe makes a vegetarian sausage and egg muffin. It is savory and a quick meal. It has two halves of an english muffin with a piece of vegan sausage and an egg in betweem.

Ingredients

  • 1 English muffin
  • 1 (or 2) Vegetarian sausage patty
  • 1 large egg
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook vegetarian sausage patty according to package directions
  2. While cooking, add olive oil and whole egg to pan
  3. Take sausage and egg out when done cooking
  4. While sausage and egg are cooking, toast english muffin

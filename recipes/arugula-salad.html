Arugula Salad

Description

This recipe makes a healthy easy salad. It is a good snack or side dish for a meal. It has fruits and veggies and lots of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl fresh arugula
  • 15-20 kalamata olive halves
  • 3-6 tablespoons feta cheese
  • 1 whole pear sliced
  • Half a can of cooked chickpeas
  • 1 whole fresh lemon
  • salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Add arugula to bowl
  2. Add chickpeas on arugula and mix
  3. Squeeze juice from lemon over salad, sprinkle on some salt and pepper, and mix
  4. Add olive halves and pear, mix
  5. Add desired amount of feta to top of salad
  6. Serve
